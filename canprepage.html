Pre-Assessment Instructions

Guidelines for the Assessment

  1. Ensure that your device is compatible and passes the system check before starting the assessment.
  2. Keep your identification proof handy for verification if required.
  3. Do not refresh or close the browser during the test. This could lead to disqualification.
  4. The test is timed. Make sure to keep track of the timer during the assessment.
  5. Use a stable internet connection to avoid disruptions.
  6. Follow all instructions carefully and do not engage in any malpractice.
