Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I check my system compatibility?

Click the "System Check" button on the assessment platform's home page to ensure your system meets the requirements for the test.

What should I do if I cannot access the camera or microphone?

Ensure you have allowed permissions for both the camera and microphone in your browser settings. Try restarting the browser and reloading the platform.

Why did my test submission fail?

Check your internet connection during submission. If the problem persists, please contact our support team through the form above.
